Student Housing Scam Simulation

Welcome! This simulation will help you learn how to rent your first home safely and avoid scams.

How it works:

  1. You will be presented with a fake housing marketplace similar to Facebook Marketplace.
  2. You can choose from a few listings, some of which may be suspicious.
  3. You will chat with a simulated landlord (AI Agent) for your chosen listing.
  4. After the chat, you'll receive a summary of your performance, highlighting good questions you asked and any missed opportunities or red flags.
